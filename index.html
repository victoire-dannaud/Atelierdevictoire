Learn more about L'Atelier de Victoire

Passionate about cooking from an early age, Victoire launched her workshops to introduce the world to the greatness of French gastronomy.

Held in Paris and Marseille, you can choose your workshop according to your level or the recipe you wish to make.

The recipes will be different according to the city, because we adopt the local recipes and products.

Fun, creativity, discovery and learning will be part of these workshops!
